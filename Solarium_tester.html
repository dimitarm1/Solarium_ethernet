REST HTTP

        Start
        http://127.0.0.1/Start/<deviceID>/<DelayTimeMinutes>/<WorkTimeMinutes>/<CoolTimeMinuntes>

        Force Start
        http://127.0.0.1/ForceStart/<deviceID>

        Force Stop
        http://127.0.0.1/ForceStop/<deviceID>

        GetStatus
        http://127.0.0.1/GetStatus/<deviceID>

http://192.168.0.150/GetStatus/16
http://192.168.0.150/Start/16/8/5/3

Тези може и да не работят, но и двете може да се симулират чрез старт с подходящи параметри
http://192.168.0.150/ForceStart/16
http://192.168.0.150/ForceStop/16
